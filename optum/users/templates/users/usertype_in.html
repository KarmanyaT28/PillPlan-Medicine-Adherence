Clear All Myths and Doubts



"an extra dose can’t possibly hurt me"

This is a Myth!

The truth is, if you take more than the dosage on the label it can hurt you.

Correct answer

"Once you feel better, you don’t have to keep taking medication."

This is a Myth!

If you stop taking your medication early, it can increase your chance of relapsing into illness.

Correct answer

"Antibiotics are the answer for every illness."

This is a Myth!

Antibiotics are only helpful in illnesses caused by bacteria, such as Strep throat.
That means antibiotics don’t work to treat many, many other illnesses and doctors must weigh very carefully whether or not to prescribe them for you.

"Medication non-adherence primarily affects the poor and undereducated"

This is a Myth!

There is no clear demographic for medication non-adherence. It can affect the CEO of a company, or the part-time janitor, although potentially for different reasons.
